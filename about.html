About me

Want to learn more about me? You're on the right page.

I am a PhD candidate at Northwestern University in the department of Biomedical Engineering, and a graduate student researcher at the Rehabilitation Institute of Chicago's Center for Bionic Medicine.

My research focuses on merging two separate fields of study:
control of robotic prosthetic hands
and
motor learning and adaptation
