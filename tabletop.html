D&D 5e Homebrew Rulesets

Travel & Exploration Lite v3.0

Travel & Exploration Lite is a lightweight ruleset for use in D&D 5e campaigns featuring exploration. The complexity and mechanical heft of other travel rules are traded for streamlined simplicity.

Crafting & Imbuing Lite v2.1

Crafting and Imbuing Lite is a lightweight ruleset for use in D&D 5e campaigns featuring item crafting. The complexity and mechanical heft of other crafting rules are traded for streamlined simplicity.

Learning & Language Lite v1.0

Learning & Language Lite is a lightweight ruleset for use in D&D 5e campaigns featuring tool and language learning.

Dueling Lite v0.3

Dueling Lite is a lightweight ruleset for use in D&D 5e campaigns featuring item crafting. The intent is to provide a simple framework for duels between characters while also allowing for strategy.
